Entanglement days of biopolymers

Virtual workshop on entangled biomolecules, June 22-26, 2021

Entanglement days of biopolymers is a hybrid event where everybody fascinated in interdisciplinary research will find something interesting.

The event consists of popular science talks, open discussions with experts in different fields of science, introductory and more advanced talks on various types of entangled biopolymers, presentations of databases, practical and technical interactive workshops. The last day is dedicated to children. Everyone interested in biomolecules, biophysics, bioinformatics, and topology is welcome to attend.
